Recipe #1

  • Prep Time: 5 mins
  • Cook Time: 5 mins
  • Serves: 4
  • Ingredients: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs, Milk, Sugar, Cinnamon, Bread, Syrup. 2, 1/4 cup, 1 tbs, 2 tsp, 4, 1 cup.

Step 1: Beat eggs in a shallow dish. Add milk, sugar, and 1 tsp of cinnamon; whisk until well blended.

Step 2: Dip each bread slice in egg mixture, turning it on both sides to evenly coat.

Step 3: Cook bread slices on lightly greased nonstick skillet on medium heat until browned on both sides.

Step 4: Mix pancake syrup and remaining 1 tsp of cinnamon in microwavable bowl. On medium 30 seconds or until heated. Serve with french toast.

Recipe #2

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Cook Time: 15 mins
  • Serves: 4
  • Ingredients: 12
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken Breasts, Olive oil, Frank's hot sauce, Paprika, Garlic power, Flour tortillas, Blue cheese or Ranch, Romaine lettuce, Carrots shredded (optional), Avocado sliced (optional), Shredded cheddar cheese. 1 pound, 2 tbs, 1/2 cup, 1/2 tsp, 1/2,tsp, 4, 1/2 cup, 2 cups, 1/2 cup, 1/2, 1/2 cup

Step 1: SLice chicken breast into long, thin strips.

Step 2: In a small bowl, whisk together oilve oil, hot sauce, paprika, garlic powder and salt. Add chicken and allow to marinate for 10 minutes.

Step 3: In a large skillet over medium high heat, add chicken strips.

Step 4: As the chicken cooks, place tortillas on a plate and spread a thin layer of bleu cheese or ranch. Layer with your lettuce, carrots, avocado, and cheese.

Step 5: Arrange a few strips down the center of each tortilla. Wrap tightly. Serve with a side of hot sauce or extra dressing. Enjoy!

Recipe #4

  • Prep Time: 15 mins
  • Cook Time: 20 mins
  • Serves: 12
  • Ingredients: 11
Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour, Sugar, Baking powder, Ground cinnamon, ground nutmeg, Kosher salt, Egg, Whole milk, Butter (melted), Vanilla extract. Topping: unsalted butter, Sugar, Ground cinnamon. 2 cups, , 1 1/2 cups, 2 tsp, 1 tsp, 1/2 tsp, 1/2 tsp, 1, 1 1/4, 2 tbs, 2 tsp. Topping: 8 tbs, 1/2 cup, 1/2 tsp

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350 degress. Spray 2 doughnut pans well.

Step 2: In a large bowl, sift together the flour, sugar, baking powder, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. In a small bowl, whish toegther the egg, milk, and melted butter, and vanilla. Stir the wet mixture into the dry ingredients until combined.

Step 3: Spoon the batter into the baking pans, filling each one a little more than three-quarters full. Bake for 17 minutes, until toothpick coes out clean. Allow to cool for 5 minutes, tap doughnuts onto sheet pan.

Step 4: For the topping, melt the 8 tablespoons of butter in an 8 inch pan. Combine the sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl. Dip each doughnut first in butter then in the cinnamon sugar.
